## Purpose (council claim)

Provide understanding of council’s approach to activities that impact negatively on:

- **Environment**  
- **Public health**  
- **Safety**  
- **Well-being**  

---

## What the policy is designed to protect / promote

Long-term sustainable management of:

1. Auckland’s **natural and physical resources**  
2. Quality **buildings, urban spaces and infrastructure**  
3. Strong, **healthy communities**  

Keywords on page: compliance policy, regulatory compliance unit, compliance framework, sustainability, non-compliance.
