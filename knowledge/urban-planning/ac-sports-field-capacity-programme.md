## Purpose (council claim)

Develop, upgrade and renew sports fields to **increase sports field capacity** across the Auckland region.

---

## Investment & need (page claims)

| Claim | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Investment | **$190m** over the **next 10 years** into developing, upgrading and renewing sports fields region-wide |
| Problem | **Shortage** of sports field capacity, **especially for training** |
| Spatial equity | Fields **not spread evenly** across the region |
| Demand target | Meet at least **80% of projected demand in all areas by 2022** |
| Funding mix | **Regionally funded** projects **and** **local board funded** projects |

**Note:** The “by 2022” demand target may be historical relative to programme updates — check whether the live page still states 2022 and what post-2022 performance reports say.

---

## Programme inclusions (page list)

- Installing **artificial turfs** and **sand carpet** fields  
- **Renewing** existing fields  
- **Lighting**  
- **Draining**  
- **New field development**  

---

## Ongoing work (page claims)

- Turf advisors work with regional sports organisations, clubs, groups and partners  
- **Annual programme review**; next year’s projects confirmed annually  
- Flexibility for: demand change, **geotechnical** issues, **soil contamination**, new turf tech, new land availability
