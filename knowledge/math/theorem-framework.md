---

## Statement vs proof

Never mix informal motivation into the formal statement. Label:

| Block | Content |
|-------|---------|
| Statement | Precise claim |
| Hypotheses | Domain, regularity, assumptions |
| Conclusion | What follows |
| Proof | Justified steps |
| OPEN | Gaps if incomplete |

---

## Proof strategies (name them)

- Direct · contradiction · induction · contrapositive · construction · ε-δ · diagram chase (with care)

One sentence **proof strategy** before the write-up.

---

## Sanity checks

- Trivial special case  
- Dimensional / scaling if analytic  
- Extremal case
