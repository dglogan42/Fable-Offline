## Local-area club blueprint (portable)

Use this when **building** a club in another suburb/city:

```text
1. Name: Knit & Natter — {Suburb}
2. Cadence: e.g. 2nd & 4th Tuesday (or weekly soft drop-in)
3. Time: 2-hour evening block (e.g. 5–7pm)
4. Venue: café / community hall / yarn shop / library meeting room
5. Cover: $0–$15 (venue drink/minimum often bundled)
6. Host: rotating or shop staff HITL
7. Channels: Meetup and/or Facebook Event + shop newsletter
8. Rules: all abilities; no hard sell; accessibility note; photo consent
9. Cancel: public post + venue if weather/illness
```

### Venue fit checklist

| Need | Why |
