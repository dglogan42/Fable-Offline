## Quick rank rubric (1–10 each, then average ≈ slay)

| Axis | 1 | 10 |
|------|---|-----|
| Light | Harsh/muddy | Flatters texture + colour |
| Focus | Soft/missed | Eyes or fit detail sharp |
| Crop | Cuts story | Intentional frame |
| Fit story | Incomplete | Head-to-toe (or deliberate crop) reads |
| Makeup read | Muddy/unfinished | Technique + cohesion |
| Energy | Checked out | Main character |
| Feed harmony | Clashes grid | Sits with recent posts |

Subjective. Two stylists can disagree — state assumptions.

## Fit (OOTD) micro-tips

- Steam or smooth obvious wrinkles if the vibe is polished  
- Check back view / tags / underwear lines if mirror full-body  
- One hero accessory > five competing ones  
- Colour: echo one accent (lip, bag, stitch) for “styled” not “random”  
- Pose: weight on one leg, angle shoulders 10–20°, chin slightly forward for most phone lenses  

---

## Makeup micro-tips (camera)

- Phone front camera softens — check a second mirror or rear camera for liner gaps  
- White balance: avoid green office cast on foundation  
- Glitter/glow: watch flash hotspots on T-zone  
- Lip edge: clean with concealer if crisp glam is the goal  
- Soft glam vs editorial: match makeup intensity to outfit volume

## Privacy (non-negotiable)

- Crop mail, lanyards, school uniforms with IDs, other people who didn’t consent  
- Think twice before real-time “I’m home alone” + location  
- Close friends list for experiments
