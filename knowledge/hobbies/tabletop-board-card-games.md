## Hobby scope

Tabletop games range from **quick small-box card games** to **large strategy board games**: party nights, two-player duels, cooperative campaigns, and deep multiplayer engines. Thousands of titles exist; pick by **player count**, **time**, **weight** (complexity), and **vibe** (party / strategy / family).

---

## Style lanes + seed titles

| Lane | What it’s for | Seed titles (examples) |
|------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Two-player strategy** | Duel nights, ~30–45 min engines | **7 Wonders Duel** (drafting + civilization) · **Jaipur** (fast trade / markets) |
| **Small-box & casual card** | Portable, simple rules, high replay | **Point Salad** · **Scout** |
| **Party & social** | Groups, silly prompts, low rules | **What Do You Meme?** · **Am I The Ass?** |
| **Broader hobby** | Engine builders, Euros, co-ops, wargames, RPGs-adjacent | Browse BGG genres — do not invent “best ever” lists offline |

Treat seed titles as **starting points**, not a closed canon. Rules/player counts change by edition — check the box or BGG.

## Session matchmaker (quick)

| Constraint | Prefer |
|------------|--------|
| 2 players, ~30 min | 7 Wonders Duel, Jaipur |
| Travel / café bag | Point Salad, Scout, other small-box |
| 4–8 people, laughs | Party / social meme-style games |
| Teach in &lt;10 min | Low weight card/party |
| Deep evening | Heavier strategy (browse BGG weight 3+ carefully) |

Ask: player count · available minutes · competitive vs co-op · teaching burden · table space.

## Play culture notes

| Topic | Guidance |
|-------|----------|
| Teach | Rules teach → short practice round → real game |
| Time | Box “30 min” often means experienced players |
| House rules | Agree before start; note for rematch |
| Accessibility | Colour-blind modes / large text vary by title — check publisher notes |
| Respect | No table flipping; pack carefully; return components |
