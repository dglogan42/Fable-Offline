## Purpose (page claim)

Learn about healthcare services and support in Aotearoa: online and phone services, when to get help, and what health professionals do. Find hospitals and services by **region**.

Also: [Which health service should I use?](https://www.healthnz.govt.nz/hospitals-services/which-health-service-should-i-use)

## Service categories linked from this page

### Healthcare providers
| Service | Role (page summary) |
|---------|---------------------|
| Public hospitals | Medical, surgical, maternity, diagnostic, emergency (varies by size/region) |
| Emergency departments | Serious illness/injury needing urgent care |
| General practices | First contact; enrol for ongoing care |
| Dentists | Care + publicly funded aspects; emergency dental |
| Sexual health clinics | Confidential advice/check-ups |
| Pharmacies | Medicines advice, OTC, prescriptions, some immunisations |
