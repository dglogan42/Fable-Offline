# Sport Auckland — Green Prescription (GRx)

**Skill:** `green-prescription-grx-kit`  
**URL:** [https://www.sportauckland.org.nz/sportauckland/green-prescription/green-prescription-grx](https://www.sportauckland.org.nz/sportauckland/green-prescription/green-prescription-grx)  
**Org:** Sport Auckland (Sporty.co.nz CMS)  
**Privacy:** `knowledge/privacy/sportauckland-grx-hosts.md`  

**Not medical advice.** GRx is a **free, referral-based** lifestyle support service — not emergency care, diagnosis, or a substitute for clinical treatment. Life-threatening symptoms → **111**. 24/7 health advice → **Healthline**. VERIFY LIVE all contacts, forms, and areas.

---

## What GRx is (page seed)

| Field | Value |
|-------|--------|
| Positioning | **A free health & wellbeing support service** |
| Model | **Referral-based** (also **self-refer** CTA on page) |
| Audience | Adults, children, young people, and **whānau** |
| Goals | More physical activity; better lifestyle; healthier eating habits |
| Delivery | Face-to-face, phone, and virtual consultations + community programmes |

### Who it may suit (page wording)

People who want encouragement/motivation to:

- Increase physical activity  
- Improve eating habits  
- Manage a **long-term health condition**  
- Support **maternal wellbeing** (pre- and post-natal)  
- Improve **mental wellbeing** if experiencing stress, low mood, anxiety, or isolation  

Approach: supportive, non-judgemental, tailored to individual goals, culture, and needs. **Free translation and interpretation** available.

## How to access (referral map)

### Sport Auckland catchment: Central / East Auckland & Counties Manukau

| Referrer type | Path (page seed) |
|---------------|------------------|
| **Primary care** | eReferrals to Green Prescription via **CareSelect** (HealthLink forms) or **Care Connect** |
| **Secondary care** | eReferrals via **Concerto** (Regional Clinical Portal) |
| **Who can refer** | All **health professionals**, **Health Coaches**, and **Health Improvement Practitioners (HIPs)** |
| **Self-refer** | Page CTA: **Click here to Self-refer** (HITL form — VERIFY LIVE target URL) |
| **Health professional form** | **Health Professional Referral Form** (HITL — VERIFY LIVE) |

Email domain seed from Outlook link: `grxreferral@sportauckland.co.nz` (use only for published purposes; VERIFY LIVE).

### West or North Auckland

| Provider | Link seed |
|----------|-----------|
| **Harbour Sport** | [https://harboursport.co.nz/](https://harboursport.co.nz/) |

Do not send West/North referrals to Sport Auckland GRx if the page says contact Harbour Sport instead.
