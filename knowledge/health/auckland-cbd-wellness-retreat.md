## Program design

### Six pillars

| Pillar | HOTC map |
|--------|----------|
| Move | Gyms |
| Flow | Yoga |
| Core | Pilates |
| Restore | Spa and Massage (+ recovery modalities) |
| Glow | Hair, nails, beauty, skincare |
| Nourish | Supplements + healthy-habit prompts |

### Three-day spine

| Day | Theme |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Arrive & unwind — spa/massage · beauty · journal |
| 2 | Move & strengthen — yoga/pilates · gym/PT · recovery |
| 3 | Integrate & glow — light movement · optional beauty · weekly habit plan |

**Express packs:** Half-day Restore · Morning Flow · Glow Afternoon · Active Reset.
