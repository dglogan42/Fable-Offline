## What TEDx Talks is

- YouTube library of talks from **TEDx** events (TED format, **independently organized** local events).  
- Scale: multi-million-subscriber channel; **cannot** be fully mirrored offline in Fable.  
- Curation strategy: **pathways** (goal → few talks → practice → Fable skill), not full index.

Related but distinct:

| Brand | URL seed |
