## Framing (from page)

Postgraduate study at UC Arts extends knowledge and applies it to complex problems. Options include certificates, diplomas, Honours, Masters, and Doctoral degrees. Mix of **career-focused taught Masters** and **research Masters/PhD**; certificates/diplomas can be flexible around work.

**CTAs:** Apply via [myUC](https://myuc.canterbury.ac.nz/login) · [Contact Arts](https://www.canterbury.ac.nz/study/academic-study/arts/contact-arts) · [Check eligibility / admission pathways](https://www.canterbury.ac.nz/study/getting-started/admission-and-enrolment/admission-pathways)
