## Accreditation & degree validation (accreditation page)

| Body / arrangement | Claim | What it is / is not (claim hygiene) |
|--------------------|-------|-------------------------------------|
| **ASIC UK** | LPU accredited; **Premier** status; certificate PDF linked | Private international accreditation service. **Not** US regional accreditation (e.g. HLC, SACSCOC, WASC). CHEA **CIQG membership** ≠ US institutional recognition of LPU degrees. |
| **EIU-Paris (European International University)** | Master’s and PhD via **CPVC** “Center & Program Validation & Certification”; graduates receive **EIU-Paris** credentials; EIU claimed authorized under French Education Act L/R 444-* as private distance education | Degree issuer may be **EIU**, not a US regionally accredited university. French “authorized private distance education” ≠ automatically equivalent to French public university *grade universitaire* for all purposes. **Verify** French MEN recognition, ENIC-NARIC, and target-country recognition. |
| **Wyoming proprietary license** | Licensed school in WY under § 21-2-401 | State **authorization to operate** as proprietary institution ≠ regional accreditation. Scope language emphasizes students **in Wyoming** — multi-state / online implications need counsel + state regs. |
| **NBHWC** | Approved training program; graduates **may sit** for NBHWC exam; CE/CPD for NBHWC | Separate from university degrees. Confirm on **nbhwc.org** approved programs list. |
| **IEAC** | Higher education accreditation body — “approved school” | Secondary/private accreditor claim — verify what IEAC is and whether it matters for your goal. |
| **IICT** | Complementary therapies methodology approval | Not a university degree accreditor. |
| **CPD Standards** | CE/CPD approval | Continuing education, not degree accreditation. |
| **Lifestyle Medicine University Foundation / HealthiWealthi** | “Approved school” | Internal/brand ecosystem — not independent higher-ed quality assurance. |
| **IBLM** | Homepage markets WORKS™ / IBLM; accreditation page: **“approval is in progress… estimated Fall 2025”** | **Tension:** marketing names IBLM while accred page says **pending**. Confirm live IBLM/ACLM pathway status; IBLM professional cert has **own** degree prerequisites (typically master’s/doctorate in health discipline from recognized institutions — see iblm.org). |

## Claim audit verdict (snapshot)

**Verdict: Marketing / partially specified — insufficient evidence for “fully accredited university degree equivalent to regionally accredited US PhD/MA”**

| Gate | Assessment |
|------|------------|
| Entity clarity | Brand clear; degree **issuer** may be EIU-Paris via validation — not always obvious on homepage “Ph.D. Degree” framing |
| Accreditation type | ASIC + private EIU arrangement + WY proprietary license ≠ US regional / national (DEAC etc.) without further proof |
| License to operate | WY claim is specific — still needs primary register check |
| Board pathways | NBHWC pathway plausible if on official list; IBLM **pending** vs homepage naming is a **red-flag inconsistency** |
| Funding figures | Unverified self-reported |
| Fast-track PhD without bachelor/master | High-risk claim for recognition — escalate to primary credential evaluation |
| Medical practice | Site disclaims diagnosis/Rx; do not treat as medical credential |

## What the user must verify next (primary sources)

1. **Wyoming** proprietary school license / authorization listing for LPU  
2. **ASIC** current accredited institutions directory + certificate dates  
3. **EIU-Paris** legal status, French MEN listing, and what the diploma actually says (issuer name)  
4. **Target country** ENIC-NARIC / credential evaluation (e.g. WES) — will they recognize EIU/LPU credentials for jobs or further study?  
5. **NBHWC** approved programs directory  
6. **IBLM / ACLM** eligibility — does *this* degree meet their master’s/doctorate requirement? Pending approval language on LPU site  
7. **State professional licensure** (if any) — health coaching regulation varies; degrees do not automatically grant medical practice rights  
8. Full **tuition, refund, scholarship terms** (not only “up to $10,000” hero)

---

## Concrete risk of trusting the page as-is

Assuming homepage “Accredited … PhD” means a **US regionally accredited research doctorate** usable for academic careers, immigration points, or board prerequisites can fail after large tuition spend — accreditation here is **ASIC + EIU validation + state proprietary license**, with IBLM approval **not** confirmed complete on the accred page.
