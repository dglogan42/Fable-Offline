## High school play framing (important)

DDLC is marketed as **psychological horror** for a general/game audience. A **high school stage play** must:

1. Follow school/district **content policies** and age of audience  
2. Use **content warnings** if dark themes appear  
3. Prefer **school-safe modes** by default (literature club comedy, friendship, poetry slam)  
4. Treat self-harm / suicide themes as **restricted** — only if adult director + policy allow; never glamorize; offer helpline footers where appropriate  
5. **Never** paste or stage the commercial game script  

### Production modes

| Mode | Use when | Content dial |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **Lit Club G** | Assembly, middle/early high school | Friendship, poems, gentle conflict |
| **Lit Club T** | HS drama club | Mystery, mild psychological unease, meta theatre (no graphic harm) |
| **Lit Club M** | Adult / approved festival only | Full psychological horror tones — still **original** script |

Default for “high school play scripter”: **G or T**.
