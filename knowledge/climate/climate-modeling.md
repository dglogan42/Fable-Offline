# Climate modeling for planners (practice module)

**Skill:** `climate-modeling`  
**Worked example:** `knowledge/climate/auckland-climate-plan.md` (Te Tāruke-ā-Tāwhiri)  
**Not climate science, engineering, or legal advice.**

---

## What “climate modeling” means in practice

| Family | Typical use | Output type |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **GHG inventory** | Account emissions by sector/year | Observed / estimated **history** |
| **Pathway / scenario models** | Explore futures under action packages | **Illustrative** or exploratory curves |
| **BAU projection** | Extend current trends without package | Counterfactual baseline |
| **Impact / hazard models** | Flood, heat, coastal, etc. | Risk maps / probabilities |
| **Global climate models (GCM) / downscaling** | Physical climate | Hazard inputs for adaptation |

Planners usually **consume** model outputs; specialist teams **run** city tools (e.g. CURB) or consultancies.

---

## Hygiene rules (always)

1. **Name the tool** (e.g. CURB + supplementary).  
2. **Baseline year** and **units** (MtCO₂e, tCO₂e/capita).  
3. **Scenario family:** BAU vs pathway vs sensitivity.  
4. **Illustrative ≠ prediction** — say so when the source does.  
5. **Sector coverage** — which sectors modelled, which not.  
6. **Uncertainty grows with time horizon**.  
7. **Residual emissions** — net zero often needs removals/tech beyond sector packages.  
8. Never invent Mt figures or “RCP/SSP” labels not in the source.  
9. Re-derive arithmetic on tables when possible (`rederive-numbers`).  
10. Separate **mitigation pathways** from **adaptation/hazard** models.

## Planner evaluation checklist for a pathway graphic

| Check | Question |
|-------|----------|
| Baseline | Against what year? |
| Boundary | City-wide? Consumption vs production? Scope 1–3? |
| Population | Are per-capita and absolute both shown? |
| BAU | What drivers (growth, fleet, energy)? |
| Actions | Which packages cut which sector bands? |
| Residual | What remains at target year? |
| Equity | Who bears transition costs/benefits? (often under-modelled) |
| Land use | Density, industrial location, transport demand feedbacks? |
| Governance | Who owns delivery vs who modelled? |
