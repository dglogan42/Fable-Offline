### Survival tips (page claims)

| Tip | Message |
|-----|---------|
| Get low | Smoke is poisonous and more deadly than flames |
| Be fast | House fire can kill in **less than three minutes**; don’t save possessions |
| Close doors | Slows fire spread |
| Get out — stay out | Don’t go back in |

**When safe:** Call emergency services, ask for **FIRE**; state address; say if anyone still inside or missing from meeting place.
